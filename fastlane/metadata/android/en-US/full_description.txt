<p><i>PocketDhamma</i> is a minimalistic offline mobile app to read the <i>Dhammapada</i> in multiple different translations. Presented in a clean, user-friendly interface without distractions or ads.</p><p><br><b>Features:</b></p><ul><li><b>Offline Support:</b> Access multiple translations of the Dhammapada without requiring an internet connection.</li><li><b>Comprehensive Search:</b> Perform full-text searches across selected translations to locate specific verses efficiently.</li><li><b>Minimalist Interface:</b> Focused on readability and clarity through a clean, distraction-free design.</li><li><b>Dynamic Theming:</b> Implements Material 3 (Material You) principles, including adaptive light and dark modes.</li><li><b>System-Integrated Styling:</b> Applies dynamic color theming for seamless integration with system-level aesthetics.</li><li><b>Cross-Platform Compatibility:</b> Built for consistent performance and appearance on both Android and iOS platforms.</li></ul>
